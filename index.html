About Me

My name is Angelica, I am a Visual Artist and a former Educator. In the past 10 years, I have created materials specifically adapted to fulfill the needs of special needs clients. I am continually enhacing my skills by attending trainings and seminars to learn about the latest strategies and evidence based programs for therapeutic Interventions. The materials my clients request are adapted for therapeutic interventions such as Speech and Language, Applied Behavior Analysis, Executive Functioning, Bilingual Education, Special and General Education.

In my free time, I like to have ride bikes and go for a swim with my family.
